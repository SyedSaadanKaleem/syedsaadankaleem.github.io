About the Game

Escape a labthat fights back.

Trapped inside an abandoned bio-lab, you have one job: find the key cards, unlock the doors, and get out. But every step you take rewrites the place around you. Corridors twist behind you. Rooms you just left aren't there anymore. The lab is alive, and the only way it changes is when you move. Keep going and you're lost. Stand still and you're never leaving. WorldScar doesn't want you out.
