Game Overview

Chess Run

You are the last King standing — and the board never ends. Chess Run is a fast-paced arcade survival where every enemy piece threatens tiles exactly like it would on a real chessboard. Knights leap in L-shapes. Bishops cut diagonals. Rooks own the lanes. One wrong step and your reign is over. Slash through enemies in a single stroke, chain massive combos, grab power-ups to transform into a Queen, Rook, Knight, or Bishop, and push deeper into the chaos. Real chess rules. Arcade chaos. How long can your reign last?
