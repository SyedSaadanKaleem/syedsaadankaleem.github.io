Game Overview

Eggi Peggi

"Eggi Peggi is a whimsical challenge where players take control of a determined chicken tasked with keeping a fragile egg from falling. Using the chicken's head, players bounce the egg into the air, navigating through increasing difficulties to ensure the egg remains airborne. The game's objective is to keep the egg from hitting the ground for as long as possible, offering a lighthearted yet engaging experience.

Simple Controls Easy-to-learn for all ages
Skill-Based Challenge Quick reflexes required
Endless Gameplay Continuous fun, rising difficulty
