Game Overview

Oneiros

Oneiros is an XR application built for a client to showcase their eyewear collection in an interactive, spatial environment. Instead of flat product photos, customers step into a scene where each pair of glasses is a real object they can reach out and handle.

Pick up a frame to inspect it from any angle. Pinch to scale it up or down. Swap its color to preview the same model across a full range of finishes. Lost track of how you've moved or resized a piece? One action snaps it back to its original position, rotation, and scale. The result turns passive product browsing into something hands-on and memorable — closer to holding the real thing than scrolling a catalog.

I led this project as Senior Developer, owning the interaction design and core XR systems: the grab-and-manipulate mechanics, the runtime scaling, the live color/material swapping, and the reset-to-default transform logic.
