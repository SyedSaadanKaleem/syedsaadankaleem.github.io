Game Overview

Swift Beat

Swift Beat combines platforming action with rhythm-based gameplay. Players control a ball that must be guided through a series of hurdles and obstacles while synchronizing with the beat of the music. The game offers a unique blend of rhythm and reflexes, where each level presents a new musical challenge and set of obstacles.

Rhythm-Based Sync moves to the beat
Platforming Twist Classic moves, musical timing
Visuals & Sound Vibrant art, upbeat tracks
Reflex Tester Fast-paced timing challenge
