Game Overview

Meteor Chase

In Meteor Chase, players take on the role of an astronaut trying to survive in a space environment filled with meteors. The game's setting is a Tic Tac Toe base, adding a unique twist to the traditional dodging gameplay. Reflexes and quick thinking are essential as players attempt to avoid the meteors and achieve the highest score.

Dodging Action

Players must dodge meteors, testing reflexes and quick decision-making.

Unique Setting

Set in a Tic Tac Toe base, offering a distinctive environment.

Skill-Based Play

Focus on avoiding obstacles for as long as possible.

Endless Challenge

Endless gameplay mode that continuously ramps up in difficulty.
